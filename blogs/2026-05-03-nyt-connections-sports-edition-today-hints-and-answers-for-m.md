<h2>What is <em>Connections: Sports Edition</em>?</h2><p>The <em>NYT</em>'s latest daily word game has launched in association with <em>The Athletic</em>, the New York Times property that provides the publication's sports coverage. The sports <em>Connections</em> can be played on both web browsers and mobile devices and require players to group four words that share something in common.</p><blockquote class="twitterEmbed twitter-tweet">

<p>Each puzzle features 16 words, and each grouping of words is split into four categories. These sets could comprise anything from book titles, software, country names, etc. Even though multiple words will seem like they fit together, there's only one correct answer.</p><p>If a player gets all four words in a set correct, those words are removed from the board. Guess wrong and it counts as a mistake &mdash; players get up to four mistakes before the game ends.</p><blockquote class="twitterEmbed twitter-tweet">
