Apple CarPlay users just got another AI assistant option for their cars: Grok. On Friday, SpaceXAI (the SpaceX's subdivision that focuses on artificial intelligence) announced that Grok's voice mode is now available in Apple CarPlay. Technically, Grok was already available in CarPlay, but it merely consisted of a placeholder app that displayed a "coming soon" message.
